Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Hair Junction operates this website and related services, including all information, content, features, tools, products, and communications, to provide you with a curated luxury hair shopping experience (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you visit, use, or make a purchase or transaction using the Services, or otherwise communicate with us.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using and accessing any of the Services, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy and understand the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described below.

Personal Information We Collect

When we use the term "personal information," we mean information that identifies or can reasonably be linked to you. Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Contact details — name, phone number, email address, billing or shipping address.
  • Order information — items you view, add to your cart, purchase, return, or enquire about, including transaction and payment details where applicable.
  • Communications — information you provide when contacting us via WhatsApp, email, contact forms, or in-store visits.
  • Device & usage information — browser type, IP address, pages visited, and how you interact with our website (including through cookies and similar technologies).

How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you when you place an order, submit an enquiry, subscribe to updates, or contact customer care.
  • Automatically when you use our website, including through cookies and analytics tools.
  • From service providers who assist with payments, delivery, hosting, or customer support on our behalf.

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide, process, and fulfil orders and enquiries (including WhatsApp checkout).
  • Communicate with you about your order, account, or support requests.
  • Improve our website, products, and customer experience.
  • Send marketing communications where you have consented (you may opt out at any time).
  • Prevent fraud, protect our business, and comply with legal obligations under South African law, including POPIA.

How We Share Information

We may share personal information with:

Our Services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us to request deletion.

Security & Retention

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We retain personal information only as long as needed to fulfil the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to certain processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable time as required by law.
